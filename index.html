Projects

Project : Painter By Numbers

This project is a Deep learning project, the primary goal of our project, is developing a model capable of determining whether two given images belong to the same artist or not.

In this project My teammate and I leveraged the power of neural networks and more specifically the power of siamese networks.

With an original Picasso carrying a 106 million dollar price tag, identifying an authentic work of art from a forgery is a high-stakes industry. While algorithms have gotten good at telling at classification of items, they aren't yet able to tell us with certainty if both paintings are by van Gogh.

and this is why we choose this project, to explore this new and exciting part of deep learning!
